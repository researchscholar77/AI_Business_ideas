Revenue Model

The primary model is ad-supported, focusing on non-intrusive contextual sponsorships with a future path to premium features. Click the legend items below to explore each revenue stream.

Contextual Sponsorships (Core)

This is the primary revenue driver at launch. Brands are offered the opportunity to sponsor video themes, background music, or visual elements that appear contextually within the AI-generated video. For example, a morning blessing video could be subtly sponsored by a jewelry brand. This model respects the user experience while providing high value to advertisers.

Premium Feature Tier (Future)

A future revenue stream will be a subscription model. This tier will cater to power users and small businesses, offering features like watermark removal, HD/4K export resolution, access to an exclusive library of high-fidelity Ganesha models and voiceovers, and the ability to generate longer videos (e.g., 60-second stories).
